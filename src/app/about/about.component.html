What is FitBuddy?

FitBuddy promotes a healthy lifestyle ranging from allowing users to keep track of their nutritional intake to searching for nutritional facts on specific food.
Users can set and edit calorie goals to meet their diet plan. Then, users can keep track of their calorie goal by adding their food consumption for the day categorized as breakfast, lunch, and dinner.
FitBuddy is a daily companion tool to encourage a healthy lifestyle starting day one.


Tech Specs

Frontend Stack: Angular, AJAX, Bootstrap, CSS, HTML, TypeScript

Backend Stack: AWS RDS, Hibernate, Java, Log4j2, Maven, SpringMVC

Testing: JUnit, Mockito, TestNG, Selenium

Deployment: AWS EC2, Git, Jenkins, SonarCloud, Tomcat
